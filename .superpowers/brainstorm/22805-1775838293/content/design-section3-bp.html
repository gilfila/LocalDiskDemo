Approval routing and payment triggers

Manager Approval Business Process
1
Worker submits registration
submitCarRegistration orch saves record (status=pending) and triggers BP on worker business object
↓
2
Routes to manager inbox
Workday BP step: Approval — routes to worker's direct manager. Shows car details + distance + eligibility in task view.
↓
✓ Approved
Orch callback updates status=approved, sets approvedDate. Worker sees confirmed status on home page.
✗ Denied
Orch callback updates status=denied. Worker sees denied status + "Register Again" button.
OTP Payment Flow (2 triggers)
🕐 Annual Scheduled (Automatic)
Nightly scheduled orchestration runs at midnight UTC
Queries all status=approved AND isEligible=true records
Filters where today == anniversary(registrationDate)
Calls issueOtp for each match → $100 payment
Updates lastOtpDate + increments otpCount
⚡ Ad-hoc (Manager-triggered)
Manager clicks "Issue OTP" on a row in manage-commuters page
Calls issueOtp orchestration for that worker
Button disabled after click + shows confirmation toast
Works regardless of eligibility (manager override)
issueOtp orchestration steps
1. Validate worker record exists + approved
2. Trigger Workday OTP Business Process ($100)
3. Update lastOtpDate = today, otpCount++
4. Return success/failure to caller
